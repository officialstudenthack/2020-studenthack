WHAT IS A HACKATHON?

Hackathons are awesome events full of creativity, technology and passionate tech-related students collaborating and creating. Attendees work in teams to create apps, games, robots… literally anything you want!

HOW MUCH DOES IT COST?

StudentHack is free for all accepted hackers. We will provide you WiFi, meals, drinks, sleeping rooms, swag,workshops, great atmosphere and… FUN!

I DON'T KNOW HOW TO CODE. CAN I STILL COME?

Hackathons are for beginners. There is an amazing suit of industry experts/mentors that will assist you!

WHAT IF I DON'T HAVE A TEAM?

No need to worry! Part of the fun at the hackathon is meeting new people. We will organise a team-building session, where you will meet other participants.

WHAT SHOULD I BRING?

All attendees must bring a valid ID for admission. Other common checklist items include laptops, chargers, sleeping bags and toiletries, spare clothes, adapters, and sleeping bags.

WHO CAN APPLY?

We accept students who are over 18 and are currently studying or have graduated less than 1 year ago. You can also join us as a mentor or volunteer! Contact us for more info.

DO YOU OFFER TRAVEL REIMBURSEMENTS?

Unfortunately, we cannot reimburse you for your travel expenses.

WHO OWNS MY IP RIGHTS, CODE AND IDEA?

You own all the rights to your intellectual property. We won’t claim any right over anything made during this event.

WHAT CAN I EXPECT FROM STUDENTHACK?

An entire weekend of great company, good food and plenty of swag! We’ll also have hardware and tons of mentors.
